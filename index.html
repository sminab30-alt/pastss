Student Information

1. Form
TenseStructureExample
Past SimpleSubject + past verb (V2 / -ed)She visited her friend.
Past ContinuousSubject + was/were + verb-ingShe was visiting her friend.

Examples:

- Past Simple: They played football.

- Past Continuous: They were playing football.

2. Use

Past Simple

  • Used for completed actions in the past.
  • I finished my homework.
  • She called me yesterday.
  • They arrived late.

Past Continuous

  • Used for actions in progress in the past.
  • Action at a specific time I was studying at 9 p.m.
  • Interrupted action I was studying when the phone rang.
  • Two actions together She was listening while I was reading.
